FAQ

Common questions

Does Findora upload my files?

No. Findora processes files locally on your Android device and does not upload files to any server.

Do I need an account?

No account is required to use Findora.

Why does Findora request file permissions?

File permissions are used only to search, organize, and manage local files on your device.

Can I revoke permissions?

Yes. You can revoke permissions at any time from Android Settings.
